About

Hi, I am Dagmawi Mekonen and I am a Developer. I try to understand how things work and improve upon them, whether it is an operating system, a program, or an object that I use every day.

My studies are mainly about Operating Systems, workflow optimizations, low-level programming specializing in Rust, and Fullstack development.

I like to keep things simple with no abstractions like the KISS principle. That's why most of my programs are easy and bare, like this website, for example. I am a heavy supporter of the suckless philosophy that I try to incorporate in every aspect of programming.
